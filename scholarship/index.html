Foundational Texts & Commentaries

The Gospel of Thomas: Sayings of Jesus

Primary Source | Nag Hammadi Library II.2

An ancient collection of sayings attributed to Jesus without narrative framework. Central to understanding Gnostic Christology.

  • Primary Text
  • Christology
  • Sayings Gospel

The Apocryphon of John: The Secret Revelation

Primary Source | Nag Hammadi Library II.1

The most important Gnostic text. Contains cosmogonic myth, creation narrative, and salvific knowledge teachings.

  • Primary Text
  • Cosmology
  • Demiurge

On the Origin of the World

Primary Source | Nag Hammadi Library II.5

Describes the creation of the world, the nature of archons, and the role of divine emanations in cosmic order.

  • Primary Text
  • Mythology
  • Archontics

Scholarly Interpretations

The Gnostic Scriptures: A New Translation with Annotations

Bentley Layton | 1987

Carefully annotated translations with extensive scholarly apparatus. Considered one of the most authoritative academic editions.

  • Translation
  • Annotations
  • Academic

The Gnostic Religion: The Message of the Alien God

Hans Jonas | 1958

Foundational philosophical analysis of Gnostic ontology, epistemology, and existential meaning. Highly influential theoretical framework.

  • Philosophy
  • Theory
  • Ontology

Gnosticism in the Second Century

Bentley Layton | 1980

Comprehensive study of Gnostic development during the crucial period of early Christian formation and fragmentation.
